Immerse

GRis Festival is a new, modern festival from Greece dedicated to showcasing artists with a fresh and innovative approach to Greek culture and heritage.

From the traditional to the avant-garde — through collaborations and the exchange of ideas, celebrating the richness of Greek culture in all its forms.
